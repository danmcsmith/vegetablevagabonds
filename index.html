About Us

Plant-Based Mobile Food Project

Vegetable Vagabonds finds the mix of a plant-based diet meets soul kitchen. Homegrown in the heart of Portland, Oregon, we cook delicious pub food sourced only from plant products. Our vibe is the energy to match your dancing feet. Creating good company, happy people, and bomb plant-based grub is the atmosphere we strive for. From music festivals to local farmers markets, make us your choice for your next event.
